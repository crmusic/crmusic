Cole Rogers Music

Independent Recording/Mix Engineer & Producer based out of Franklin, TN

Throughout his life, Cole has aspired to make a great impact on music just as it has upon him. He started out playing the drums at the age of 8, and from there taught himself acoustic guitar and ventured into the piano as well. At 16, he began writing & recording his own songs on a Zoom 8 track analog recorder. From there his desire to go deeper into recording music only grew. In the fall of 2010, he began school in the Recording Industry Program at Middle Tennessee State University. Through the program he took close to 35 classes teaching over various in depth topics such as: music theory, studio and electrical signal flow, production technology, pro tools, managing small and large businesses and more. In 2013 he interned for one of Nashville’s commercial studios on music row, Loud Recording. In 2014 he interned for a recording studio in East Nashville- The Stu- Ready, Set, Record, LLC. He collaborated with various artists such as Cherub, Chris Young, and The Black Cadillacs. Throughout his four and a half years of studying audio production, he connected with several local bands and artists recording and mixing their projects as well as writing, recording, and mixing his own.

After graduating from MTSU in the fall of 2014, he is now located in Franklin, Tennessee where he continues to work as a recording/mix engineer and producer. For production, recording, or mixing services, you can contact Cole via phone or email, just click on the “Contact” headline above!
